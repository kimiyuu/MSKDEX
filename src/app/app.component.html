ms. K D Explorer

one who loves to explore and escape
I am Kim Ilustrisimo . Kimmy at home and Ms. K in school. I am a licensed professional teacher by profession, student and a traveler by heart and soul. I work 7am to 5pm on weekdays, and 6pm to 10pm as a student, now a traveler on my free time and days.
It’s hard – traveling knowing that I’ve got tons of activities to check when I get back to school, forms to accomplish, grades to compute, reports to be submitted, bills I have to pay and a portfolio to defend. It’s suffocating that I realized one day, I needed an escape.
I went out to see what this world has laid on my feet. I stepped out of the threshold to explore the wonders of what’s dubbed as ‘Pearl of the Pacific’ has to offer. I met a lot of people. I made new friends. I got oriented with different cultures and languages. I tasted food I have never tried before. I’ve done things I never thought I would do. I was brought by my wandering sole to places I’ve never thought of visiting. I was infinite for a moment and sky was the only limit I had. Life is meant to be lived colorfully, in a way you want it to be, in a way that you will enjoy it. I intend to color mine with my adventures – people, food, places, everything!
